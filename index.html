VENTURE ENGINEERING

ABOUT

Venture Engineering was founded in 2006 by John Munson, P.E.. The company is native of the Reno-Sparks area with more than 30 years of extensive experience in the planning, design, and management of land development. Venture Engineering has completed a variety of projects across Northern Nevada while providing professional services to Architects, Developers, Home Builders and General Contractors, as well as many private individuals and businesses. The company provides services that encompass all phases of project development from initial site investigation, preliminary design and entitlement to final design, permitting and construction.
